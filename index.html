About Us

"Choose a job you love and you will never have to work a day in your life!" We are enthusiastic developers trying to help students by providing them a platform for proper career guidance.We provide test for self analysis which will help students to pick up the right career option according to their personality and liking. In career dendrogram we will provide all the career options keeping in mind their previous degrees and if students still have any queries about choosing their career they can contact professionals of that particular field. Our future plans include building professionals section where professionals can login and solve the queries of the students. We also plan to provide some demo courses in future. These courses will give a brief practical idea about any degree. Students can go for these demo courses and practically check if their decision is right or not. They can check whether actually studying that particular subject is fun or not in these demo courses and be sure about their future plans
